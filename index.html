What are Lithophanes?

Lithophanes are essentially embossed photos generated by a 3D printer. The way it works is that light passes through the thin parts while being blocked by the thick parts. In this way, the differences in light bring out the details of the image.

Turning photos into 3D objects gives them more feeling and literally adds a new dimension. Of the various ways to do this, lithophanes are a simple way to enhance your favorite photos.
